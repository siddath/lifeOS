# 02_Areas — the standing parts of your life

While [`01_Focus/`](../01_Focus/) holds the one mission you're pushing *now*, this folder holds the parts of life that are always true and rarely change: your direction, your guardrails, your routines, and a searchable knowledge base about you.

Think of it as the **slow layer**. You revisit these during weekly and quarterly reviews, not daily.

## Templates in this folder

Every file ships as a `*.template.md` with `<!-- lifeos:fill -->` markers. Copy each to its plain name (e.g. `north_star.template.md` → `north_star.md`) and fill it in.

| Template | What it captures |
|----------|-----------------|
| [`north_star.template.md`](north_star.template.md) | Your direction — what a good life looks like, what's non-negotiable, what's noise. |
| [`one_year_vision.template.md`](one_year_vision.template.md) | Where you want to be in twelve months, per area. |
| [`house_vision.template.md`](house_vision.template.md) | A concrete picture of the home / environment you're building toward. |
| [`panic_parking_lot.template.md`](panic_parking_lot.template.md) | A protocol for anxious spirals: write the thought, label it, convert it to one action. |
| [`life_audit.template.md`](life_audit.template.md) | An honest snapshot of where each area stands today. |
| [`wellbeing_routine.template.md`](wellbeing_routine.template.md) | Your physical / mental baseline — sleep, movement, calm. |
| [`relationships.template.md`](relationships.template.md) | The people who matter and how you show up for them. |
| [`routines.template.md`](routines.template.md) | Daily / weekly / monthly / yearly recurring commitments. |
| [`hobbies.template.md`](hobbies.template.md) | What you do for joy, not output. |

## knowledge_base/

A twelve-part [`knowledge_base/`](knowledge_base/) — a calm, searchable profile of *you*, so any AI session (or future you) can look up one fact without re-deriving your whole life. Each file is a short prompt-scaffold. Fill the ones that matter first; you don't need all twelve on day one.

See [`../examples/alex/`](../examples/alex/) for filled versions.
