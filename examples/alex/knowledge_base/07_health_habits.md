# 07 — Health & Habits

> Alex's physical and mental baseline, and the keystone habits.

## The base I protect

Sleep first (7+ hours — recall and mood both fall apart below it), the morning walk, and one calm block a day. If a hard week only leaves room for those three, that's still a good-enough day.

## Keystone habits

- **Morning walk** — the anchor. Clears the head before the screen. Rarely missed.
- **Read 20 min** — usually with coffee or before bed. Keeps the mind out of work-only mode.
- **Workouts, 3×/week** — the one that slips first. Working on making it non-negotiable, not aspirational.
- **Weekly call with parents** — Sundays.

## Physical / training

Aiming for three real workouts a week — a mix of gym and a longer weekend walk or bike. During launch crunch, the floor is: keep the daily walk, don't drop below two workouts.

## Mental / calm practice

The morning walk doubles as the calm practice — no podcast, just walking. On stressful days, a few minutes of breathing before opening the laptop. Pottery on Saturdays is its own kind of meditation.

## Where I struggle

Workouts are the honest weak spot — they're the first thing sacrificed to a deadline, and then a good week never quite comes back to catch up. The smallest version that still counts: a 20-minute session. Do that instead of skipping entirely.
